About Me

I am a British 2D artist looking for roles such as Concept Artist, Character Concept Artist, and related positions.
I also have roots in Illustration and love crafting detailed, highly-rendered illustrations for posters, cover art, and visdev purposes etc..
However my main area of expertise is Concept Art, particularly for, but not limited to, Characters and Cosmetics.

Why Me?

At the time of writing (05.2024) I have just completed my Illustration BA at University, and so I lack experience working for studios in a professional capacity.
But this also means I am not set in the ways of any one studio or style, and can learn and adapt to your company's values.
I can offer you and your company a new and fresh perpective - one that is eager to work and learn to the very best of its ability. I will challenge myself every day to exceed expectations; to not only meet the high standard of today's concept art and design, but to raise it.
